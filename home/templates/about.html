URL Shortener

This is a URL shortener made in Django, which utilizes SQLite as its database and offers a convenient way to shorten long URLs. By leveraging Django's powerful framework, developers can create a robust and scalable application. The use of SQLite as the database ensures efficient storage and retrieval of shortened URLs. With this setup, users can easily generate short URLs and track their usage, making it a valuable tool for managing and sharing links.
